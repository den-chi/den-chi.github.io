Transfer Pricing

Transfer pricing is the setting of the price for tangible assets sold, transferred and used, intangible assets transferred and used, manpower supplying, financing, etc. between related parties.

With the transfer pricing regulation being introduced in China and relative rules and bylaws are being perfected, Chinese Tax Administration continually strengthens the investigation in transfer pricing and considers it as one of the emphases at work.

Denchi Advisory Co. Ltd not only gains affluent practical experiences in China, but also actively shares the transfer pricing developments all around China with clients. We offer transfer pricing services including preparing transfer pricing documents, making plans and optimized proposals, investigating, defending and making disputes and advanced pricing agreements (APA).

Transfer pricing document preparation, planning and optimized proposals

The experienced experts in Denchi can assist your company to fluently prepare transfer pricing same-period documents in China. While Denchi is preparing same-period documents, we will consider both your company’s actual situation and tax agents’ requirements, making the documents to support the most of your company’s transfer pricing policy.

At the meanwhile, transfer pricing planning is the effective and essential measure for companies to control transfer pricing risks actively in the non-arm’s length transactions. We analyze the related-party transactions by understanding the company’s operations, function and risk, assets and corresponding costs, expenses and profits arrangements; we will figure out the potential problems and risks in the related-party transactions from transfer pricing aspects and make professional suggestion to the problems. Finally, we discuss with the managements of the future transfer pricing processes as recommendations.

Transfer pricing investigating, defending and making disputes

Denchi can assist your company:

  • Analyze your company’s financial situation, transfer pricing policy and come up with optimized defending strategy favors your company;

  • Represent your company to explain the transfer pricing proposition of your company and assist your company to negotiate with Tax Agency.

Denchi and the Group-CTAC Group have the multiple opportunities to communicate with State Administration of Taxation and local taxation bureaus at different levels. At the meanwhile, our experts have abundant experiences in transfer pricing investigation, defense and disputes.

Advanced Pricing Agreements (APA)

Denchi helps corporations take adequate precautious procedures and make agreements with one or more tax agencies about transfer pricing method. Therefore, companies are able to plan in advanced regarding to crucial transfer pricing issues like risks of double taxation. In recent years, CTAC Group goes beyond the Big Four in terms of applying and negotiating APA issues..

The experts in Denchi and CTAC Group have been working on APA for years, with rich practical experiences. Among them, Mr. Yukang Wang, the CTAC Group senior partner, was former Deputy Director of International Tax Department of State Administration of Taxation (SAT). He was in charge of the international tax cooperation, tax treaties, tax information exchange, international tax anti-avoidance, and advanced pricing agreements (APA). More specifically, he led and participated in the regulations negotiating and planning of the above-mentioned areas, took responsibility for the quality of investigating and closing of the international anti-avoidance cases, as well as engaged in planning the advanced pricing agreements with Japan, America and Korea, respectively; having a positive influence on them.
