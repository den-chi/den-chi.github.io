Enterprise Restructuring

Regardless of new company setup, acquisition, alteration, merger, splitting or liquidation, all kinds of businesses involve complicated issues. These require experienced professionals to be committed and dedicated. To address various financial problems, taxation issues, legalization, compliance, integration and human resources concerns involving in the businesses, Denchi Advisory Co., Ltd aims to provide enterprises with professional and intermediary services, assist enterprises to mitigate potential risks and ensure enterprises to fully achieve their desired operational objectives.

Denchi Learning

Better tell him how to make a buck than just give him one. Denchi learning shares our experiences of corporation structure, transfer pricing, tax, customs and foreign exchange in Chinese and Japanese.

Transfer Pricing Service

Denchi Advisory Co. Ltd not only gains affluent practical experiences in China, but also actively shares the transfer pricing developments all around China with clients. We offer transfer pricing services including preparing transfer pricing documents, making plans and optimized proposals, investigating, defending and making disputes and advanced pricing agreements (APA).

Tax and Business Advisory Service

Denchi can assist your company to lower the risks in terms of tax, customs and foreign exchange, therefore company can achieve the balance between reducing tax cost and tax compliance and deepen the understanding of tax regulations and practices in China.
