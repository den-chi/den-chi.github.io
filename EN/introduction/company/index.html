About Us

Denchi Advisory Co., Ltd.(Denchi) Is one of the leading consulting companies in China. We provide comprehensive tax and business advisory services to multi-national and Chinese clients in terms of a broad range of international and domestic transactions.

Denchi’s headquarter locates in Shenzhen, having an affiliate in HongKong-New Sun Consulting Co., Limited. We also have partners in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Suzhou, Tokyo, Seoul, Singapore, and San Francisco and so on.

Denchi has experienced experts. Our team members’ outstanding capabilities are not only presented in the abundant experiences in comprehensive consulting services across multiple business areas; but also shown in the full support to clients regarding to our comprehensive advisory services.

Either our traditional advantageous business, such as TP documents preparation, planning and optimized suggestions, enterprise taxes, customs, foreign exchange, corporate tax incentives, corporate tax declaration, corporate tax health check, due diligence, and employee dispatching and taxation; or our emerging business, like enterprise restructuring, distant training, transfer pricing, investigating, defending and making disputes, advanced pricing agreements, internal control for corporate taxation, tax treaty negotiation and so on; Denchi has always been in the leading position in the tax and business consulting industry.
