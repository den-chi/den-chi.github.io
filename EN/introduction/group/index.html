About CTAC Group

Denchi is one of the main members of China Tax Advisory Company Group (“CTAC Groups”).

CTAC Group is an organization comprised of many members, locating in Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Tokyo, Seoul, Singapore, San Francisco etc., which engages in providing high-quality tax and business advisory services and strived to become one of the leading tax and business consulting organizations.

All team members in CTAC Group specialize in law, tax and business. Among them, the senior partner of CTAC Mr. Yukang Wang was former Deputy Director of International Tax Department of State Administration of Taxation (SAT). Mr. Wang was mainly in charge of international tax cooperation, tax treaties, tax information exchange, international tax anti-avoidance, and advanced pricing agreements (APA), etc.

Another senior partner Mr. Ning Song is a CTA Certified member, council member of China CTA Committee and China Fiscal-Tax Law Institute, as well as the holder of a LL. M in Taxation and Master Diploma of Economics.

Mr. Song has been a senior partner of Transfer Pricing department in one of the Big Four Accounting Firm, leading the TP business in North China. Before that Mr. Song has held a position in State Administration of Taxation for a long time, participating in drafting important documents such as , , and Chapter six of < Enterprise Income Tax Law (revised)> and its implementing regulations.
