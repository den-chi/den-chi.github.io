2008-09-23

Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Tax Policy Issues Relating to Pre-tax Deduction Standards for the Payment of Interest by Enterprises to Affiliated Parties

Cai Shui [2008] No.121

September 23, 2008

To the financial departments (bureaus), state taxation bureaus and local taxation bureaus of all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, and cities specifically designated in the state plan and the financial bureau of the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps:

In order to regulate the pre-tax deduction of interest to be paid by enterprises and strengthen the administration of enterprise income tax, in accordance with

Article 46 of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Enterprise Income Tax (hereinafter referred to as the "Tax Law") and Article 119 of the Regulations on the Implementation of the Enterprise Income Tax Law of the People's Republic of China (Order of the State Council No. 521, hereinafter referred to as the "Implementation Regulations" ), policy issues relating to the pre-tax deduction of interest to be paid by enterprises after accepting credit investments of affiliated parties are hereby set out as follows:

Article 1 In the assessment of the taxable income of an enterprise, any portion of interest actually paid by the enterprise to the affiliated parties concerned that fails to exceed ratios specified hereinafter or ratios specified in relevant provisions of the Tax Law and the Implementation Regulations shall be allowed to deducted and any portion thereof that exceeds such ratios shall not be deducted for the current period or in the following years.

In addition to complying with the provisions of Article II hereof in the actual payment of interest to the affiliated parties concerned, an enterprise shall observe the following criteria for the ratio between credit investments and equity investments it accepts from the affiliated parties:

  1. In the case of a financial enterprise, 5:1; and

  2. In the case of any other enterprise, 2:1.

Article 2 Where an enterprise can provide relevant information in accordance with relevant provisions of the Tax Law and the Implementation Regulations and prove that relevant transaction activities comply with the independent transaction principle or the tax burden of such an enterprise is not heavier than that of a domestic affiliated party, interest actually paid by the enterprise to the domestic affiliated party shall be allowed to be deducted in the assessment of the relevant taxable income.

Article 3 For any enterprise that engages in both financial and non-financial business, interest actually paid by any such enterprise to the affiliated parties concerned shall be calculated separately by way of reasonable methods; where separate calculation by way of reasonable methods fails to be carried out, the ratio relating to any other enterprise specified in Article I hereof shall be followed without exception to calculate interest to paid that is allowed to be deducted on a pre-tax basis.

Article 4 For any interest income that an enterprise obtains from the affiliated parties concerned in an improper manner, the enterprise shall pay enterprise income tax in accordance with relevant provisions.
