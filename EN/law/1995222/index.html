Circular of the Ministry of Finance on Relevant Provisions Concerning Financial Management in Foreign-invested Enterprises under Liquidation

Cai Gong Zi [1995] No. 222

July 9, 1995

To the finance departments (bureaus) of all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government and cities specifically designated in the state plan and all departments of the State Council:

For the purpose of reinforcing the financial management work in foreign-invested enterprises under liquidation, relevant issued are hereby prescribed as follows:

Article 1 Foreign-invested enterprises shall conduct financial management during liquidation period in accordance with relevant provisions of laws, regulations of the state and this Circular and shall accept finance authorities' check and supervision.

Article 2 Where foreign-invested enterprises declare termination according to the provisions of contract, articles of association or for other reasons, they shall form a liquidation committee within 15 days to organize and carry out liquidation work. Members of the liquidation committee are usually composed of directors and representatives of creditors, and also Chinese certified public accountants or lawyers may be hired and competent finance authorities may appoint person to participate in if they consider necessary.

When enterprises are declared bankrupt, the liquidation procedure shall be commenced.

Article 3 The liquidation committee shall be responsible for formulating liquidation program, balance station and other accounting station, catalogue of assets and list of credit and debt, put forward disposal opinions of assets and report to the board of directors for approval and implement after reporting to competent finance authorities and competent authorities in charge of enterprises for filling.

The liquidation committee shall declare liquidation and notify creditors and the creditors shall declare credits within specified time limit.

Article 4 Assets of enterprises under liquidation include all assets of the enterprise when liquidation is declared and capital obtained during the check and calculation period. Unless it is otherwise provided for by the state, assets under liquidation shall not include the following three parts ("Three Parts of Assets"):

(1) Employee bonuses and welfare funds, housing circulating funds balanced for Chinese employees and all properties and facilities purchased or built up by using these two funds;

(2) Insurance and welfare fees balanced for Chinese employees; and

(3) Trade union expenses balanced and properties purchased or built up by using these expenses.

Article 5 Enterprises' assets shall be used to pay off debts in an order as specified by finance systems on the industry and enterprise basis, among which, land use fees, price allowance for Chinese employees and other finance revenue shall be handed over to finance authorities in the same order as tax of China.

Article 6 Enterprises (limited liability companies and companies limited by shares) with the status of Chinese legal person shall use all of their assets to assume paying-off liabilities for debts; while as for enterprise without the status of Chinese legal person, their investors shall assume joint and several liabilities for paying-off debts of the enterprises.

Where Chinese-foreign cooperation enterprises stipulate in the contract that the foreign party may recover investment firstly within the cooperation term and all fixed assets will be attributed to the Chinese party when the cooperation term expiries, the Chinese and foreign party shall assume liabilities for the enterprises' debts when the enterprises are under liquidation.

Article 7 The balance of enterprises' assets in liquidation with all debts and losses, as well as, the enterprises' undistributed profits, capital reserves, all equity funds and liquidation fees deducted, in excess of the paid capital is liquidation incomes and shall be considered as profits, on which corporate income tax shall be paid. The remaining assets after cooperate income tax is paid shall be distributed to the investors in accordance with the following principles: as for Chinese-foreign joint venture enterprise, actual contribution percentage of the investors; as for Chinese-foreign cooperation enterprises, stipulations in the contract or articles of association; and the articles of association as for wholly foreign-owned enterprise.

Article 8 When a foreign-invested enterprise accomplishes the liquidation and the foreign party withdraws its investment, the Chinese party shall conduct corresponding finance disposal for the remaining assets it obtains in accordance with its nature, form and sources. And Three Parts of Assets as specified in Article 4 hereof shall be handled in accordance with the following different circumstances:

(1) Where the enterprise is operated continuously by the Chinese party, Three Parts of Assets shall still be used by the enterprises, among which, employee bonuses and welfare funds balanced and all properties purchased or built up by using these funds shall be combined into the public welfare fund, housing circulating funds balanced for Chinese employees and all properties and facilities purchased or built up by using these two funds; insurance expenses balanced for Chinese employees shall be combined into the employee welfare expense; housing circulating funds balanced and all properties purchased or built up by using these funds shall be combined into the housing circulating funds; and trade union expenses balanced and properties purchased or built up by using these expenses shall be transferred into the trade union account.

(2) When an enterprise is dissolved, if the Chinese party include two or more enterprises and one or two of them intend to withdraw its (their) equity, Three Parts of Assets shall be remained in the enterprises in which there are a majority Chinese employees to be used in accordance with provisions, and the specific distribution percentage shall be determined through negotiation between all Chinese parties. And

(3) When the foreign-invested enterprise terminates, Three Parts of Assets shall be handed over to the receiving party to be used in accordance with provisions.

Article 9 After the liquidation is completed, liquidation report set forth by the liquidation committee together with liquidation statements and accounts report of Chinese certified public accounts shall be reported to competent finance authorities within 15 days and cancellation formalities of financial registration shall be gone through.

Article 10 After the liquidation of an enterprise is completed, prior to the cancellation formalities, the liquidation program, files and relevant financial and accounting materials of a Chinese-foreign equity joint venture or cooperative joint venture shall be kept by the Chinese party or the competent authorities in charge of enterprises and those of a wholly foreign-owned enterprise shall be kept by finance authorities.

Article 11 Finance authorities of each level shall strengthen the financial management on foreign-invested enterprise under liquidation and shall impose penalty on enterprises that fail to legally conduct financial work according to the Supplementary Provisions on the Implementation of New Enterprise Financial Systems by Foreign-invested Enterprises.

Article 12 This Circular shall come into effect as of the date of promulgation.
