Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues Concerning the Administration of Tax Rebates (Exemptions) on Exported Goods and Services

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2014] No. 51

August 28, 2014

In order to further improve the administration of export tax rebates (exemptions), relevant issues are hereby announced as below:

Article 1 The implementation of Article 3 and Article 9 of the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues Concerning Providing Exchange Collection Documents for Export Tax Refund (Exemption) Declaration by Export Enterprises (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2013] No. 30) shall cease. The circumstances, specified in Article 2 thereof, where an export enterprise must provide the foreign exchange collection receipts for exported goods in the event of declaring a tax rebate (exemption) are divided into the following five categories upon adjustment:

  1. being listed by a foreign exchange administrative department as an enterprise of C class;

  2. being listed by the customs as an enterprise of C or D class;

  3. being assessed by the tax authority as an enterprise with tax credit grade of Level D;

  4. the competent tax authority discovers that the reason declared by the export enterprise for its failure to collect foreign exchange is false; and

  5. the competent tax authority discovers that the foreign exchange collection receipts for exported goods provided by the export enterprise are counterfeit.

Article 2 With regard to a multinational corporation that implements collective operation of foreign exchange funds upon approval by the foreign exchange administrative department, its member corporations may, within an approved valid period, declare any tax rebate (exemption) on R&D and design services provided to the outsiders at the competent tax authority by presenting receipt vouchers that are issued by the banks to the corporation that carries out collective operation of the funds of the multinational corporation and meet the following provisions, and no longer provide the materials specified in Item 3 of Subparagraph 3 of Paragraph 5 of Article 13 of the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on the Issuance of the Administrative Measures for Applying the Tax Refund (Exemption) to Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated Value-added Tax (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2014] No. 11).

  1. The payers are the overseas entities that conclude the R&D and design contracts with the member corporations, or the overseas paying agents stipulated in the R&D and design contracts; and

  2. The payees stated in the receipt vouchers or the actual payees stated in the postscripts must state the names of the member corporations.

Article 3 With regard to a project that is constructed through international bid invitation by means of utilization of the loan from an international financial organization or foreign government, the bid inviter shall, when applying to the competent tax authority at the place where it is located for issuing the Bid-winning Certification Notice, provide the Circular on Alternative Projects on Foreign Government Loans issued by the finance department or the original copy of the Reloan Agreement on the XX Project on Loans from XX Bank (International Financial Organization) signed between the finance department and the department or government in charge of the project and the photocopy thereof stating that it is identical with the original (the original is returned after the original and photocopy ones are certified identical by the competent tax authority), and no longer provide the Notice on Bid Evaluation Results made by the National Bid Evaluation Committee. Where the Notice on Bid Evaluation Results made by the National Bid Evaluation Committee has been provided previously, the Bid-winning Certification Notice may be handled according to the previous provisions.

Article 4 This Announcement becomes effective as of the date of promulgation. With regard to export tax rebate (exemption) declared before the date, the provisions of this Announcement may apply.

It is hereby announced.

国家税务总局

2014年8月28日
