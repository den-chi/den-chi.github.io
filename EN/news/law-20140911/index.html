Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning the Monitoring and Administration of Special Tax Adjustments

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2014] No.54

August 29, 2014

In accordance with the relevant provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Enterprise Income Tax and the implementing regulations thereof, issues concerning the monitoring and administration of special tax adjustments are hereby announced as follows:

Article 1 Where a tax authority finds, by such means of monitoring and administration of special tax adjustments as review of related declaration, administration on materials of the same period, preliminary monitoring and control as well as follow-up tracking and administration, that a taxpayer is exposed to risks of special tax adjustments, it shall send to the taxpayer a Notice on Taxation Matters, remind it or him of the risks of special tax adjustments and require it or him to provide materials of the same period or other relevant materials within 20 days in accordance with the relevant provisions. The taxpayer shall review and analyze the rationality of such special tax adjustments as its or his pricing principles and methods of related party transactions and may adjust and make up taxes at its or his discretion. Where a taxpayer requires the tax authority to confirm such special tax adjustments as the pricing principles and methods of related party transactions, the tax authority shall, in accordance with the relevant provisions, start the special tax adjustment and investigation procedures, determine a reasonable adjustment method and carry out taxation adjustments.

Article 2 Where the taxpayer adjusts and makes up taxes at its or his discretion in the phase of the monitoring and administration of special tax adjustments, the tax authority may implement special tax investigations and adjustments in accordance with the relevant provisions.

Article 3 Where the taxpayer provides materials of the same period and other relevant materials in accordance with the above provisions in the phase of the monitoring and administration of special tax adjustments, in accordance with the provisions of Paragraph 2 of Article 122 of the Implementing Regulations of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Enterprise Income Tax, the interest on taxes made up will be charged at an interest rate of the RMB benchmark lending interest rate published by the People's Bank of China for the year in which tax payment occurs. 5% will be no longer separately charged.

It is hereby announced.
