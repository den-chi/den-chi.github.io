Why Denchi

Denchi Advisory Co., Ltd was founded on 2013 in Shenzhen. Our three founders all once worked in the tax and business advisory department in the big four accounting firm (the “Big Four”).

“Denchi” in Japanese is a homophone of “Spreading Wisdom” and “Battery”, meaning “Spreading Wisdom and Share Success”, aiming at empowering our clients’ success.

Our firm is the Shenzhen office of China Tax Advisory Company Group (“CTAC Groups”). The network of CTAC covers both domestic and overseas, including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Seoul, Japan, San Francisco and etc.

We gather the experienced people from the Big Four, aiming at recruiting the best people, forming the best team, providing high-quality, high-efficient and valuable business and tax advisory services in China and becoming the top consulting services institution in the industry. At the meantime, we provide talented people with the opportunities of challenged work and sense of achievement.

What will you obtain in Denchi？

If you go to a newly founded company in your 20s, you will learn faster than anyone.

If you have a dream in working in the professional consulting industry at the meanwhile, please join us!

Here, you can learn from the founders who were graduated from Beijing Foreign Studies University/ Sun Yat-Sen University/ Guangdong University of Foreign Studies with 10-year working experiences.

Here, you will obtain the opportunities to fully show your talents and improve yourself in multi-dimension.

Here, you will be paid more than what you can get in the Big Four.

We believe that only when we take good care of our employees, they will take good care of our clients.

Only treating employees with sincerity can form a friendly working environment.

We are committed to create a favorable atmosphere- help each other, grow up together. We wish our employees can feel the joy of work and grow at work.
