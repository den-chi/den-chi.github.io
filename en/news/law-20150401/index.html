Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Adjusting Matters relating to the Administration of General Taxpayers of Value-added Tax

2015-04-01

（Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2015] No.18）

According to spirit of the Decision of the State Council on Cancelling and Adjusting a Batch of Administrative Examination and Approval Items (Guo Fa [2015] No.11), the State Administration of Taxation has made adjustments to matters relating to the administration of general taxpayers of value-added tax (hereinafter referred to as the "VAT") that are hereby announced as follows:

Article 1 General VAT taxpayers (hereinafter referred to as the "general taxpayers") are subject to the qualification registration system, and VAT taxpayers (hereinafter referred to as the "taxpayers") apply to the competent tax authorities thereof for handling registration matters.

Article 2 Taxpayers shall follow the following procedures when applying for qualification registration of the general taxpayer:

  1. the taxpayers fill out the Qualification Registration Form for General Taxpayer of Value-added Tax (Appendix I) and submit the same, along with a tax registration certificate, to the competent tax authorities;

  2. if the contents filled by the taxpayers are consistent with the tax registration information, the competent tax authorities shall make registration on spot; and

  3. if the contents filled by the taxpayers are inconsistent with the tax registration information, or do not meet the filling requirements, the tax authorities shall inform the taxpayers of the contents that need to be added and corrected.

Article 3 If taxpayers whose annual taxable sales amount exceeds the standards prescribed by the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation (hereinafter referred to as the "prescribed standards") meet the provisions of relevant policies, and select to pay taxes as small-scale taxpayers, the taxpayers shall submit a written explanation (Appendix II) to the competent tax authorities.

Any individual, other than individual businesses, whose annual taxable sales amount exceeds the prescribed standards does not need to submit a written explanation to the competent tax authorities.

Article 4 Where taxpayers' annual taxable sales amount exceeds the prescribed standards, the taxpayers shall handle the relevant procedures in accordance with the provisions of Article 2 or 3 of the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Adjusting Matters relating to the Administration of General Taxpayers of Value-added Tax (hereinafter referred to as the "Announcement") within 20 working days after the end of the declaration period; if the taxpayers fail to handle the procedures within the specified time limit, competent tax authorities shall make a Notice on Tax Affairs within ten working days after the end of the specified period, informing the taxpayers that the latter shall handle related formalities within ten working days.

Article 5 Unless otherwise provided by the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation, taxpayers calculate the taxes payable based on the general VAT calculation method and receive special VAT invoices for use in accordance with the provisions from the effective date of the general taxpayer qualification chosen thereby.

Article 6 The Announcement shall come into force as of April 1, 2015. Item 1, Paragraph 2 of Article 4, and Articles 7, 8, 9 and 11 of the Administrative Measures for the Qualification Recognition of General Value-added Tax Payers (Order of the State Administration of Taxation No.22) are suspended, and the corresponding provisions will be republished after revised in accordance with the prescribed procedures.

It is hereby announced.
