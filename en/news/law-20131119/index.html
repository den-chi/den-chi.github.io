Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues Concerning Value-added Tax for Asset Restructuring of Taxpayers

2013-11-19

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2013] No.66

November 19, 2013

The issues concerning value-added tax for asset restructuring of taxpayers are hereby announced as follows:

In the event that after a taxpayer conducts several transfers of all or part of its physical assets as well as debt-claims and debts related thereto through combination, division, sale and replacement in the course of asset restructuring, the ultimate transferee and labor force receiver are the same entity or individual, the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues Concerning Value-added Tax for Asset Restructuring of Taxpayers (the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2011] No.13) shall still apply and such transfers of goods shall not be subject to value-added tax. The assets transferor shall submit the asset restructuring scheme and other relevant documents and materials to the competent tax authority.

The Announcement shall come into force as of December 1, 2013. The matters in respect of the taxpayer which have occurred and been treated will no longer be adjusted; the untreated matters shall be subject to the provisions hereof.

It is hereby notified.

State Administration of Taxation
