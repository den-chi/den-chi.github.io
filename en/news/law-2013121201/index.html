2013-12-12

Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on the Inclusion of the Railway Transport Industry and Postal Service Industry in the Pilot Collection of Value-added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax

Cai Shui [2013] No.106

December 12, 2013

Finance departments (bureaus), the state administration of taxation and local taxation bureaus of all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, and cities separately designated in the state plan and the Financial Bureau of Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps,

The railway transport industry and postal service industry are included in the pilot collection of value-added tax in lieu of business tax (hereinafter referred to as the "BT to VAT") with approval of the State Council. Considering the problems reflected in pilots of value-added tax in lieu of business tax in transportation industry and certain modern services industries, we modify and perfect the policies for pilots of BT to VAT. Now relevant pilot policies are printed and released for your implementation in compliance.

Article 1 The pilot BT to VAT for railway transport industry and postal service industry shall be nationally carried out as of January 1, 2014.

Article 2 All the above mentioned departments shall attach great importance to and effectively enforce the organization and leadership of the pilot with well-planned organization, thorough arrangement, and clear and definite duty allocation. Various effective measures shall be taken to make all-round preparation before the pilot and the monitoring and analysis and propaganda and interpretation in course of pilot so as to ensure a steady, orderly and smooth reform. In case of any problem, please report to the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation in time.

Article 3 For the implementation date hereof, unless otherwise stated, the matters specified in the appendices to the Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on the Inclusion of the Railway Transport Industry and Postal Service Industry in the Pilot Collection of Value-added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax shall come into force as of January 1, 2014. The Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Tax Policies in the Nationwide Pilot Collection of Value Added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax in the Transportation Industry and Certain Modern Services Industries (Cai Shui [2013] No.37) shall be abolished as of January 1, 2014.
