Circular of the State Council on Printing and Issuing the Reform Proposals for the Registered Capital Registration System

Guo Fa [2014] No.7

February 7, 2014

The people's governments of all provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities directly under the Central Government and all ministries and commissions of the State Council and all institutions directly under the State Council,

The State Council has approved the Reform Proposals for the Registered Capital Registration System (hereinafter referred to as the "Proposals"), which are hereby printed and issued.

Article 1 The reform of industrial and commercial registration system and the promotion of the facilitation of industrial and commercial registration system are material decisions of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council. The reform of registered capital registration system is an important measure, which deeply implements the spirits of the 18th National Congress of the CPC and the second and third plenary meetings of the 18th National Congress of the CPC, and is significant for speeding up the functional transformation of governments, innovating regulation methods of governments, establishing fair, open and transparent market rules and guaranteeing ventures and innovations.

Article 2 As the reform of registered capital registration system is extensive and largely depends on policies, the people's governments at different levels shall reinforce organization and guidance, and coordinate with the solutions of specific problems during the reform. All regions and all departments shall cooperate closely and speed up formulating and improving implementing measures. The administrations for industry and commerce shall optimize processes and improve systems so as to ensure the stable transition of management work before and after the reform. Self-management of enterprises, self-discipline of industrial associations and supervision functions of social organizations shall be strengthened and market regulation level shall be enhanced so as to practically implement the reform, further release reform bonuses, inspire venture vitalities and generate new development motivations.

Article 3 Relevant administrative regulations and decisions of the State Council are modified in accordance with the decision of the Standing Committee of the People's Congress on revising the Company Law and the Proposals. Specific provisions shall be otherwise announced by the State Council.

The State Administration for Industry and Commerce shall report material issues arising during the implementation of the Proposals to the State Council in time.

Reform Proposals for the Registered Capital Registration System

In order to actively and steadily promote the reform of registered capital registration system, the Reform Proposals for the Registered Capital Registration System (hereinafter referred to as the "Proposals") are hereby formulated in accordance with Proposals of the State Council for Institutional Reforms and Functional Transformation.

Article 1 Guidelines, Overall Objectives and Basic Principles

  1. Guidelines.

Hold high the great banner of socialism with Chinese characteristics, take Deng Xiaoping Theory, important thought of Three Represents and scientific outlook on development as guidelines, insist on the reform direction of socialist market economy, impel the reform of registered capital and other registration matters of companies, propel the reform of supporting regulation system, perfect modern enterprise system and serve sustainable and sound development of economic society according to requirements for speeding up functional transformation of governments and building up service-oriented governments.

  1. Overall Objectives.

Through the reform of registered capital and other registration matters of companies, further relax the control of market subjects access, reduce access thresholds, optimize operation environments and promote market subjects to speed up development; through the reform of supervision and administration system, further transform supervision and administration methods, strengthen credit supervision and administration, promote synergic supervision and administration and improve supervision and administration effects; through intensifying the information announcement of market subjects, further enlarge social supervision, facilitate social shared governance, inspire various market subjects to create vitalities and strengthen endogenous power of economic development.

  1. Basic Principles.

(1) Convenience and high efficiency. According to requirements of proper conditions, simple procedures and low costs, facilitate applicants' handling of the registration of market subjects. Encourage investments and ventures, innovate service methods and enhance registration efficiency.

(2) Standard and unification. Implement unified registration procedures, registration requirements and basically equal registration matters for various market subjects, standardize registration conditions and materials and reduce the intervention in autonomous matters of market subjects.

(3) Loose access and strict administration. While extending the access conditions of registered capital and others, further intensify the responsibilities of market subjects, perfect and improve supporting supervision and administration system, consolidate the supervision and administration over market subjects and promote the construction of social integrity system so as to maintain the market order of loose access and fair competition.

Article 2 Relax the Access Control over Market Subjects and Effectively Optimize the Business Environment

  1. Implement the registered capital subscription registration system. The total capital contributed by shareholders of companies or the total share capital subscribed by promoters (namely, the registered capital of companies) shall be registered with the administrations for industry and commerce. Shareholders (promoters) of companies shall independently agree upon the contribution amounts, contribution methods, contribution terms and others and record the same in the articles of association thereof. Shareholders of companies with limited liability shall take responsibility for companies subject to their subscribed contribution amounts, while shareholders of companies limited by shares shall take responsibility for companies subject to shares subscribed by them. Companies shall publicize contribution amounts subscribed by shareholders or shares subscribed by promoters, contribution methods, contribution terms and payment to the public through the system of credit information announcement system of market subjects. Shareholders (promoters) of companies shall be responsible for the truthfulness and legitimacy of their contributions.

Extend registration conditions of registered capital. Except that any law, administrative regulation and the decision of the State Council otherwise stipulate the minimum of registered capital of special industries, cancel the restrictions of minimal registered capital of CNY30,000 for companies with limited liability, that of CNY100,000 for one-person companies with limited liability and that of CNY5 million for companies limited by shares. Relax the restrictions on the initial contribution proportions of all shareholders (promoters) at the time of incorporation of companies or the proportion of monetary contributions to registered capital of all shareholders (promoters) of companies, or the period for shareholders (promoters) of companies to pay up contributions.

Paid-up capital of companies shall not be continually taken as an industrial and commercial registration matter. At the time of registration, companies do not need to submit capital verification reports.

Study and decision on issues concerning the implementation of registered capital subscription registration system shall be made otherwise if any existing law, administrative regulation and the decision of the State Council specifically stipulate that banking financial institutions, securities companies, futures companies, fund management companies, insurance companies, special insurance agencies and insurance brokers, direct selling enterprises, foreign labor service cooperation enterprises, financing guarantee companies, companies limited by shares established through public offer, labor dispatch enterprises, pawnshops, insurance assets management companies and small-amount loan companies shall implement the registration system of paid-up registered capital. Existing provisions shall be complied with before the amendment to the aforesaid law, administrative regulation and the decision of the State Council.

Individual proprietorship enterprises, partnership enterprises and farmers' professional cooperatives that have implemented declared (subscribed) contribution registration shall conform to existing provisions.

Encourage, guide and support non-company enterprises as legal persons such as state-owned enterprises and collective enterprises to implement the standard reform of company system and registered capital subscription registration system

Actively study and explore the industrial and commercial registration of new market subjects.

  1. Reform the annual inspection and license examination system. The annual inspection system of enterprises shall be changed to annual report announcement system of enterprises. Enterprises shall within prescribed period by year, submit annual reports to the administrations for industry and commerce through the system of credit information announcement of market subjects, publicize them to the public and make them available to any entity and individual. Main contents of annual reports of enterprises shall include contributions of shareholders (promoters) and assets status of companies. Enterprises shall be responsible for the truthfulness and legitimacy of annual reports, and the administrations for industry and commerce shall selectively examine the publicized contents in annual reports of enterprises. If it is found that annual reports of enterprises conceal facts and are false upon examination, the administrations for industry and commerce shall impose sanctions in accordance with the law, and report the information about legal representatives and principals of enterprises to relevant departments of public security, finance, customs and taxation. The administrations for industry and commerce shall record enterprises which fail to publicize annual reports within prescribed periods in operation abnormality list on the system of credit information announcement of market subjects so as to remind the said enterprises to fulfill the obligation of publicizing annual reports. Enterprises which fulfil the obligation of publicizing annual reports in three years may apply to the administrations for industry and commerce for restoring normal record status; if not, the administrations for industry and commerce shall permanently record the said enterprises in the operation abnormality list, shall not restore their normal record status and shall record the enterprises in the list of enterprises with serious violations (hereinafter referred to as the "Blacklist").

Reform the license examination system for individual businesses, and establish the annual report system consistent with characteristics of individual businesses.

Explore and implement the annual report system for farmers' professional cooperatives.

  1. Simplify domicile (operating premise) registration formalities. Applicants may be registered if they submit the lawful use certification of premises. As for domicile (operating premise) conditions of market subjects, the people's governments of all provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities directly under the Central Government may independently formulate or authorize the people's governments at lower levels to formulate specific provisions in accordance with the laws and regulations and according to the actual requirements of management of their own regions and in the principle of facilitation of access of market subjects and effective guarantee of economic and social orders.

  2. Implement electronic business license and whole-process electronic registration management. Establish industrial and commercial registration digital certification management system under Internet environment, and proactively implement the electronic business license consistent with national unified standards in order to provide identity authentication and electronic signature service guarantee for electronic government affairs and electronic commerce. Electronic business license which has industrial and commercial registration information shall have the same legal force as paper business license. Greatly implement the whole-process electronic registration management method supported by electronic business license of online application, acceptance, review, announcement and license issue and so on, and enhance the informatization, facilitation and standardization of registration management of market subjects.

Article 3 Implement Strict Supervision and Administration over Market Subjects and Lawfully Maintain the Market Order

  1. Establish the credit information announcement system of market subjects. Improve the credit information announcement system of market subjects. Establish the credit information announcement system of market subjects based on the national information resources database of enterprises as legal persons to back up the construction of social credit system. On the credit information announcement system of market subjects, the administrations for industry and commerce shall publicize the information about registration, filing, supervision and administration of market subjects; enterprises shall submit and publicize annual reports and licenses of obtaining qualifications according to provisions; annual reports and licenses of obtaining qualifications of individual businesses and farmers' professional cooperatives may be publicized on the system according to provisions. Publicized contents shall be taken as important bases for relevant departments to carry out administrative licensing and supervision and administration. Strengthen the management of announcement system, establish service guarantee mechanism and provide convenient services for relevant units and the public.

  2. Perfect credit constraint mechanism. Establish the system of operation abnormality list. Market subjects which fail to publicize annual reports within prescribed period and cannot be contacted by registered domiciles (operating premises) shall be recorded in operation abnormality list and be publicized to the public on the credit information announcement system of market subjects. Further impel the management and application of the "Blacklist", and improve the punishment mechanism for breaking faith with employment restrictions to legal representatives and principals of enterprises as legal persons. Linkage and response mechanisms shall be established, and relevant departments shall take targeted credit constraint measures for market subjects which are recorded in operation abnormality list or the "Blacklist" and have other records of violation of law and relevant principals thereof in order to form the situation that "violation in one aspect leads to restrictions in all aspects". Establish and improve the overseas claim guarantee mechanism, record overseas investors and actual controllers thereof that do not fulfill subscription obligation and have fraud and violation behaviors in "key monitoring list", and strictly examine or restrict the investment in China which may be made by them in any way in future.

  3. Consolidate judicial relief and criminal punishment. Specify the administrative duties of governments on supervision and administration over market subjects and market activities, and distinguish the boundary between civil disputes and administrative disputes. Respect civil rights of market subjects, and the administrations for industry and commerce shall implement formal examination for application materials in industrial and commercial registration. In case that any industrial and commercial dispute incurs civil dispute between shareholder and company or between shareholders, the parties concerned shall institute civil proceedings in the people's court to seek judicial relief. Support and coordinate the people's court to fulfil civil trial functions and hear the cases about economic disputes such as equity dispute and contract dispute according to law in a bid to protect the legitimate rights and interests of the parties concerned. Where the parties concerned or interested parties concerned require the handling of industrial and commercial registration according to the valid judgment document or the notice of assistance in execution of the people's court, the administrations for industry and commerce shall handle the registration in accordance with law. Give full play to the punishment and deterrent effects of criminal justice on crimes, and relevant departments shall actively coordinate with public security organs, procuratorial organs and the people's court to fulfil duties and punish crimes damaging the order of socialist market economy in accordance with law.

  4. Give play to the supervision and self-discipline functions of social organizations. Enhance the participation in industrial association, give play to their effects including industry management, supervision, constraint and professional ethics construction, and guide market subjects to fulfill contribution obligations and social responsibilities. Actively give play to effects of professional service institutions such as accounting firms and notary organizations, and strengthen the supervision over market subjects and behaviors thereof. Support industrial associations, arbitration institutions and other organizations to solve disputes between market subjects through mediation, arbitration, award and other methods. Proactively cultivate and encourage the development of social credit evaluation institutions, support the development of credit rating, and provide objective and fair enterprise credibility information.

  5. Strengthen self-management of enterprises. Implement registered capital subscription registration system. As for the adjustment for basic systems, companies shall perfect self-management methods and mechanism, improve internal governance structure, give play to the supervision effects of independent directors and supervisors, and strengthen their own responsibilities. Shareholders (promoters) of companies shall be clear of the responsibility for registered capital subscription, rationally make subscription promises, strictly assume actual contribution responsibilities according to the time, amount and others set out in articles of association and relevant agreement.

  6. Strengthen the supervision and administration over operation behaviors of market subjects. Strengthen the supervision over the access and withdrawal behaviors of market subjects, vigorously impel the execution of anti-unfair competition and anti-monopoly, and consolidate the standard management over different commodities transaction markets, and maintain the market order of fair competition. Consolidate commodities quality supervision and administration, severely crack down the infringement upon the exclusive right to use trademarks and sales of fake quality commodities, strictly investigate and solve false and illegal advertisements, severely attack pyramid sales and strictly regulate direct sales so as to safeguard legitimate rights and interests of operators and consumers. All departments shall fulfil supervision and administration duties within their function scopes, intensify inter-departmental coordination, and form the work pattern of clear work division, smooth communication and comprehensive emphasis and management, therefore enhancing supervision and administration efficiency.

  7. Strengthen the management over domiciles (operating premises) of market subjects. According to complaints and reports, the administrations for industry and commerce shall solve the inconsistency between the registered domiciles (operating premises) of market subjects and actual conditions in accordance with law. Departments of planning, construction, land, house management, public security, environmental protection, safety supervision and administration and so on shall, in accordance with law, manage the market subjects which should have domiciles (operating premises) with specific conditions or make use of illegal buildings or arbitrarily change house purposes to conduct operation activities; in case of involvement of permission and approval matters, the administrations for industry and commerce responsible for permission and approval shall carry out the supervision and administration in accordance with law.

Article 4 Guarantee Measures

  1. Strengthen organization and leadership. The reform of registered capital registration system involves many departments, is extensive and intensive in policies. According to the unified deployment of the State Council, the local people's governments at various levels shall perfect unified leadership thereof, performance of respective duties and cooperation of all departments, and concentrate the strengths of all parties to coordinate the work mechanism of promoting the reform. Mobilize the power of personnel of first-line windows to ensure convenient and efficient registration. Relevant departments shall speed up the formulation and improvement of supporting supervision and administration systems, coordinate the promotion of the systems, simultaneously implement them and intensify subsequent supervision and administration. Establish and perfect inter-departmental information communication and sharing mechanisms, credit information disclosure mechanisms and case supporting investigation and delivery mechanisms, and intensify synergy and supervision and administration. Superior departments shall strengthen guidance and supervision, research and solve problems arising from the reform in time, and coordinate synergy to put forward the reform.

  2. Speed up informatization construction. Make full use of informatization methods to enhance the capabilities of collection, integration and services of basic information and credit information of market subjects. In the principle of "physical dispersion, logical concentration and differential shielding", speed up the construction of unified and standard credit information announcement system of market subjects. All provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities directly under the Central Government shall take the concentrative and unified credit information announcement system of market subjects of local regions as the preconditions for the reform of the said region. Administrations for industry and commerce shall optimize and perfect industrial and commercial management informatization system to ensure the steady transition of industrial and commercial registration management businesses before and after the reform. Relevant departments shall actively promote the innovations in political services, establish work mechanisms and technological environments for departments to synergically handle political affairs targeted at market subjects, and enhance the comprehensive efficiency of political services. People's governments at various levels shall make more investments to provide necessary personnel, facilities and fund guarantee for the informatization construction of establishing the credit information announcement system of market subjects, implementing electronic business license and so on.

  3. Improve legal guarantee. Actively promote unified business registration legislation, speed up the improvement the laws and regulations about access and supervision and administration of market subjects, establish credit information announcement and management systems of market subjects, prevent market risks and guarantee transaction safety. All regions and departments shall according to the amendments to laws and regulations, conduct the "establishment, amendment and cancellation" work of relevant regulations and normative documents according to the arrangements of the State Council.

  4. Stress publicity guide. Insist on correct guidance of public opinions, make full use of various media, well publicize and explain the policies on the reform of registered capital subscription registration system, answer and respond to hot issues with social attention in time, guide the public to correctly understand the significance of the registered capital registration system and the contribution responsibility of shareholders, fully understand the effects of the credit information announcement system of market subjects, widely participate in the construction of integrity system, form a good atmosphere of understanding, caring for and supporting the reform to ensure the smooth promotion of the reform.
