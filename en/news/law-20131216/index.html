Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Matters Concerning the Recognition of the Qualification of General Taxpayers of Value-added Tax in the Pilot Collection of Value-added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax

2013-12-16

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Matters Concerning the Recognition of the Qualification of General Taxpayers of Value-added Tax in the Pilot Collection of Value-added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2013] No. 75

December 16, 2013

In order to ensure the smooth implementation of the pilot collection of value-added tax ("VAT") in lieu of business tax (hereinafter referred to as "VAT in lieu of business tax"), the matters concerning the handling of recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT for the taxpayers that are included in the scope of the pilot projects for VAT in lieu of business tax (hereinafter referred to as "Pilot Taxpayers") are hereby announced as follows:

Article 1 Pilot Taxpayers shall handle the recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT in accordance with the provisions hereof.

Article 2 Unless in the circumstances prescribed in Article 3 hereof, Pilot Taxpayers with annual sales turnover of taxable services exceeding 5 million yuan prior to the pilot implementation of VAT in lieu of business tax (hereinafter referred to as "prior to the pilot implementation") shall apply for handling the procedures for recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT with the competent taxation authority of the State Administration of Taxation (hereinafter referred to as the "Competent Taxation Authority").

The annual sales turnover of taxable services of Pilot Taxpayers prior to the pilot implementation shall be converted according to the following formula: the annual sales turnover of taxable services=total business turnover of taxable services for a consecutive period of less than 12 months?(1 3%).

For Pilot Taxpayers from which VAT is collected after deduction in accordance with the existing provisions on business tax, their business turnover of taxable services shall be calculated according to the business turnover prior to deduction.

Article 3 Pilot Taxpayers who have acquired the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT prior to the pilot implementation with taxable services are not required to re-apply for recognition. The Competent Taxation Authority shall prepare and serve on the Notice on Taxation Matters and inform the taxpayers.

Article 4 Pilot Taxpayers with annual sales turnover of taxable services not exceeding 5 million yuan prior to the pilot implementation may also apply for recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT with the Competent Taxation Authority if they meet the relevant prescribed conditions.

Article 5 Prior to the pilot implementation, branches of the State Administration of Taxation at the provincial level may formulate pre-recognition measures for Pilot Taxpayers for recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT.

Article 6 After the pilot implementation, qualified Pilot Taxpayers shall handle the recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT in accordance with the Administrative Measures for the Qualification Recognition of General Value-added Taxpayers (Order of the State Administration of Taxation No. 22) and relevant provisions. Pilot Taxpayers which may be subject to deduction in determining the sales turnover in accordance with the provisions of VAT in lieu of business tax, the annual sales turnover of taxable services shall be calculated according to the sales turnover prior to deduction.

Article 7 Pilot Taxpayers who also sell goods, provide processing, repair, maintenance and labor services and taxable services shall separately calculate the sales turnover from taxable goods and labor services as well as the sales turnover from taxable services, the standards for recognition of the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT shall separately apply.

Entities and individually-owned industrial and commercial businesses that also sell goods, provide processing, repair, maintenance and labor services and taxable services with non-frequent taxable activities may opt for taxation payment in accordance with small-scale taxpayers.

Article 8 Where Pilot Taxpayers who have acquired the qualification of general taxpayers of VAT have committed acts of VAT evasion, deception of export duty rebate or false issue of VAT deduction receipt, the Competent Taxation Authority may exercise administration on the Pilot Taxpayers during taxation payment guidance period of not less than six months.

Article 9 This Announcement shall come into effect as of January 1, 2014. The Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Matters Relating to the Determination of the Qualifications of General VAT Taxpayers in the Pilot Collection of Value-Added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax in Shanghai (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2011] No. 65), the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Matters Concerning the Qualification Recognition of General Value-added Taxpayers in the Pilot Collection of Value-added Tax in Lieu of Business Tax in the Transportation Industry and Certain Modern Services Industries (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2013] No. 28) and the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Matters Relating to Confirmation of Qualification as General Value-Added Taxpayers (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2013] No. 33) shall be repealed simultaneously.

Announcement is hereby made.
