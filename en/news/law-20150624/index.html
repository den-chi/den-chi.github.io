Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Several Issues concerning the Collection Administration of Enterprise Income Tax in Enterprise Restructuring Business

2015-06-24

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2015] No.48

The issues concerning the collection administration of enterprise income tax in enterprise restructuring business are hereby announced as follows in accordance with the relevant provisions of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Enterprise Income Tax and its implementing regulations, the Law of the People's Republic of China on the Administration of Tax Levying and its implementing regulations, the Decision of the State Council on Cancelling Non-administrative Licensing Examination and Approval Items (Guo Fa [2015] No.27), the Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning Treatment of Enterprise Income Tax in Enterprise Restructuring Business (Cai Shui [2009] No.59), the Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning the Enterprise Income Tax Treatment for the Promotion of Enterprise Restructurings (Cai Shui [2014] No.109) and other relevant provisions:

Article 1 According to the restructuring type, the parties involved in enterprise restructuring refer to:

  1. The parties involved in a debt restructuring refer to the debtor and the creditor.

  2. The parties involved in an equity acquisition refer to the acquirer, the transferor and the acquired enterprise.

  3. The parties involved in an asset acquisition refer to the acquirer and the transferor.

  4. The parties involved in a merger refer to the merging enterprise, the enterprise to be merged, and the shareholders of the enterprise to be merged.

  5. The parties involved in a division refer to the new/surviving enterprise after the division, the enterprise being divided, and the shareholders of the enterprise being divided.

In the aforesaid restructuring deals, the transferor in an equity acquisition, the shareholders of the enterprise to be merged in a merger, and the shareholders of the enterprise being divided in a division may be natural persons.

For taxation of the natural persons of all parties concerned, the relevant provisions of the individual income tax shall apply.

Article 2 If the parties involved in restructuring which are enterprises apply the special tax treatment (which means that the restructuring business conforms to the requirements specified in Circular Cai Shui [2009] No.59 and Articles 1 and 2 of Circular Cai Shui [2014] No.109 and the special tax treatment is adopted, hereinafter the same), the leading party to the restructuring shall be determined according to the following provisions:

  1. For debt restructuring, the leading party shall be the debtor.

  2. For equity acquisition, the leading party shall be the equity transferor. In case there are two or more equity transferors, the leading party shall be the party who transfers the largest ratio of the equities in the acquired enterprise shall be the leading party. (If the ratios of the equities transferred are the same, the leading party may be determined through consultation).

  3. For asset acquisition, the leading party shall be the asset transferring party.

  4. For merger, the leading party shall be the enterprise to be merged. In case there are several enterprises under common control to be merged, the leading party shall be the party who owns the largest net assets.

  5. For division, the leading party shall be the enterprise being divided.

Article 3 The current year in which the restructuring business is completed as mentioned in Article 11 of Circular Cai Shui [2009] No.59 refers to the tax year for the enterprise income tax in which the restructuring day falls.

The enterprise restructuring day is determined according to the following provisions:

  1. For debt restructuring, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the debt restructuring contract (agreement) or the court ruling enters into effect.

  2. For equity acquisition, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the assignment contract (agreement) enters into effect and the equity change procedures are completed. For equity acquisition between related enterprises, and if the equity change procedures have not been completed within 12 months after the assignment contract (agreement) takes effect, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the assignment contract (agreement) takes effect.

  3. For asset acquisition, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the assignment contract (agreement) takes effect and the parties concerned conduct the accounting treatment.

  4. For merger, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the merger contract (agreement) enters into effect, the parties concerned conduct the accounting treatment and complete the formalities for the business registration of new incorporation or change. For a merger for which it is not necessary to go through the formalities for the business registration of new incorporation or change, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the merger contract (agreement) takes effect and the parties concerned conduct the accounting treatment.

  5. For division, the restructuring day shall be the day on which the division contract (agreement) enters into effect, and the parties concerned conduct the accounting treatment and complete the formalities for the business registration of new incorporation or change.

Article 4 For enterprise restructuring business to which the special tax treatment is applicable, except for the circumstance under which simple changes in other legal forms occur in an enterprise as specified in Item 1 of Article 4 of Circular Cai Shui [2009] No.59, the parties involved in restructuring shall respectively submit the Report Form and Attachments for Special Tax Treatment of Income Tax in Enterprise Restructuring (see Appendix I for details) to the competent tax authorities and the declaration materials (see Appendix II for details) when handling the annual tax declaration for the enterprise income tax of the year when the restructuring business is completed. The restructuring party in a merger and division involving cancellation shall conduct declaration before going through the formalities for cancelling the tax registration.

After the declaration made by the leading party in restructuring, other parties concerned handle the tax declaration with their respective competent tax authorities, with the photocopies of the Report Form and Attachments for Special Tax Treatment of Income Tax in Enterprise Restructuring upon acceptance of the competent tax authority of the leading party submitted.

Article 5 For enterprise restructuring business to which the special tax treatment is applicable, at the time of tax declaration, it should be explained that the enterprise restructuring is of bona fide commercial purposes in the following aspects item by item:

  1. the ways of restructuring transaction;

2.the substantive results of restructuring transaction;

  1. changes in tax conditions of the parties involved in the restructuring;

  2. changes in financial conditions of the parties involved in the restructuring; and

  3. information on the participation of non-resident enterprises in restructuring activities.

Article 6 For enterprise restructuring business to which the special tax treatment is applicable, at the time of tax declaration, the parties concerned shall also submit a statement to their respective competent tax authorities on whether there are any other equity or asset transactions related to the restructuring within 12 consecutive months before the restructuring, and explain whether such transactions and the restructuring constitute the step transactions and whether they have been treated as one enterprise restructuring business.

Article 7 According to the provisions of Article 10 of Cai Shui [2009] No.59, if one restructuring business involves the step transactions conducted within 12 consecutive months and spans two tax years, and the parties concerned anticipate that the whole transaction complies with the conditions for application of the special tax treatment after the transaction is completed in the first tax year, and such parties agree to adopt the special tax treatment through consultation, the special tax treatment may temporarily apply and the declaration materials shall be submitted in written form when the parties concerned declare the annual enterprise income tax for the relevant year.

After the whole transaction is completed in the next tax year, the enterprises should judge whether the special tax treatment is applicable. If the special tax treatment applies, the parties concerned should submit the relevant materials according to the requirements of Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Several Issues concerning the Collection Administration of Enterprise Income Tax in Enterprise Restructuring Business (hereinafter referred to as the "Announcement"). If the general tax treatment applies, the annual declaration form for enterprise income tax of the corresponding tax year shall be adjusted for the calculation and payment of enterprise income tax.

Article 8 Where an enterprise runs into debt restructuring as stipulated in Item 1 of Article 6 of Circular Cai Shui [2009] No.59, the enterprise shall accurately record the income from debt restructuring which shall be recognized, and, at the time of final settlement of enterprise income tax for the corresponding year, explain the recognized amounts of the relevant year and the amounts carried forward by year.

The competent tax authorities shall establish an account and conduct comparison and analysis of the account and the income from debt restructuring as declared by the enterprise every year, to strengthen the follow-up management.

Article 9 Where debt restructuring as stipulated in Item 3 of Article7 of Circular Cai Shui [2009] No.59 occurs, a resident enterprise shall accurately record the income from debt restructuring which shall be recognized, and, at the time of final settlement of enterprise income tax for the corresponding year, explain the recognized amounts of the relevant year and the amounts carried forward by year.

The competent tax authorities shall establish an account, and conduct comparison and analysis of the tax basis of the equity acquired by the resident enterprise and the income from asset or equity transfer as recognized every year, to strengthen the follow-up management.

Article 10 The enterprises to which the special tax treatment is applicable, when conducting the restructuring asset (equity) transfer or disposal in subsequent years, should make a special explanation on the income or loss from the asset (equity) transfer when they make the annual tax declaration, including information on the comparison between the tax basis of restructuring asset (equity) as determined for the special tax treatment and the tax basis for assets (equity) transfer or disposal, and the treatment of deferred income tax liabilities.

For an enterprise to which the special tax treatment is applicable, and when conducting the restructuring asset (equity) transfer or disposal in subsequent years, the competent tax authorities should strengthen the assessment and examination, and compare the tax basis of the restructuring asset (equity) as determined for the special tax treatment and the tax basis for the transfer or disposal of the asset (equity) as well as the relevant annual tax returns. If any problem is found, it should be adjusted in accordance with the law.

Article 11 Tax authorities shall do well in the statistics and archiving of the relevant materials relating to the enterprise restructurings to which the special tax treatment is applicable. The offices of the State Administration of Taxation and local taxation bureaus of all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government and cities specifically designated in the state plan should, before the end of August each year, submit the Statistical Form for Special Tax Treatment of Income Tax in Enterprise Restructuring (see Appendix III for details) to the State Administration of Taxation (the Income Tax Department).

Article 12 The Announcement applies to the final settlement of annual enterprise income tax for 2015 and subsequent years. Articles 3, 7, 8, 16, 17, 18, 22, 23, 24, 25, 27, and 32 of the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Promulgating the Administrative Measures for Enterprise Income Tax on Enterprises' Restructuring Business (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2010] No.4) shall be repealed simultaneously.

If an enterprise has already signed a restructuring agreement but has not yet completed the restructuring upon the effectiveness of the Announcement, the Announcement shall apply.

It is hereby announced.
