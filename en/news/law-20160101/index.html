Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning Further Strengthening the Interim and Ex-Post Administration of Export Tax Rebates

2016-01-01

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2016] No. 1

Effective date：January 7, 2016

In order to implement the Proposals for Deepening the Reform of Systems for Tax Collection and Administration of SAT Authorities and Local Taxation Authorities, further strengthen the interim and ex-post administration of export tax rebates (exemption) and continue to improve services for tax rebates, relevant matters, based on the problems and proposals reported from different places, are hereby notified as follows after discussion,

I. Where a group company declares purchased goods as self-made goods for the tax exemption, offset and refund, the head office thereof or the manufacturing enterprise held by the group company, when filing with the competent tax authorities, don't need to submit the copies of Tax Refund (Exemption) Filing Form (or the Form for Recognition of Export Tax Refund (Exemption) Qualification) of the group company's head office and its holding manufacturing enterprise.

II. Where an export enterprise or any other entity that applies for withdrawal of an export tax rebate (exemption) record, has declared to the competent taxation authority for forgoing undeclared export tax rebate (exemption) or declared export tax rebate (exemption) not yet handled and applies for tax exemption according to provisions, it shall be deemed having settled export tax rebate payment. Where an export enterprise or any other entity (hereinafter referred to as the "Withdrawal Enterprise") that applies for withdrawal of a export tax rebate (exemption) record due to merger, division, restructuring and reorganization, it may provide the following materials to the competent taxation authority. After all the materials are verified by the competent tax authority without any mistake, it shall be deemed to have settled export tax rebate payment.

  1. Confirmation of Non-settlement of Tax Rebate (Exemption) by Enterprises Applying for Withdrawal of an Export Tax Rebate (Exemption) (See Appendix 1);

  2. Resolution on Merger, Division or Restructuring and Reorganization of Enterprises, Articles of Association, and Approval Documents of Relevant Departments; and

  3. Name of the account opening bank and account number set up at the domicile of the withdrawal enterprises by enterprises that succeed to the rights and obligations of the withdrawal enterprises (hereinafter referred to as the "Succeeding Enterprise").

After the withdrawal is concluded, the rebate amount for the withdrawal enterprise shall be returned to the account of the succeeding enterprise by the competent taxation authority. And in case of any excessively rebated amount, the competent taxation authority may seek such excessive amount from the succeeding enterprise.

III. Where foreign trade enterprises export imported goods, they shall not provide the Declaration Forms for Imported Goods any longer when declaring tax rebate (exemption).

IV. The Application Form for Certificate of Tax Exemption on Processing with Imported Materials (See Appendix 2), Certificate of Tax Exemption on Processing with Imported Materials (See Appendix 3), Application Form for Certificate of Goods Import Agent (See Appendix 4) and Certificate of Goods Export Agency (See Appendix 5) issued together with this Announcement shall be put into use as of the promulgation date of this Announcement. Appendix 28, appendix 29 and appendix 33 attached to the Administrative Measures for Value-Added Tax and Consumption Tax on Exported Goods and Services (Announcement of State Administration of Taxation [2012] No. 24) shall be repealed simultaneously.

V. This Announcement shall come into force as of the promulgation date. Sub-item 3 of Item 6 in Article 3 in the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on the Administrative Issues concerning the Cancellation of the Administrative Examination and Approval Process for Certain Tax Matters (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2015] No. 56), provisions that declaration of goods for import shall also be provided indicated in the sub-item 5 (2) and (5) of Item 2 in Article 5 of the Administrative Measures for Value-Added Tax and Consumption Tax on Exported Goods and Services shall be repealed simultaneously.

It is hereby notified.
