Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on the Issuance of the Administrative Measures for Applying the Tax Refund (Exemption) to Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated Value-added Tax

2014-02-08

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on the Issuance of the Administrative Measures for Applying the Tax Refund (Exemption) to Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated Value-added Tax

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2014] No.11

February 8, 2014

For the purpose of implementing the policies and provisions in respect of the zero-rated value-added tax for taxable services in the collection of business tax in lieu of value-added tax (hereinafter referred to as the "VAT"), the State Administration of Taxation, with the approval of the Ministry of Finance upon consultation, has formulated the Administrative Measures for Applying the Tax Refund (Exemption) to Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated Value-added Tax, which are hereby promulgated for implementation as of January 1, 2014. The Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Promulgating the Administrative Measures for Tax Refund (Exemption) for Taxable Services Subject to Zero Value-added Tax Rate (Interim) (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2013] No.47) shall be repealed simultaneously.

It is hereby announced.

Administrative Measures for Applying the Tax Refund (Exemption) to Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated Value-added Tax

Article 1 Any taxable services which are eligible for zero-rated VAT and provided by general VAT payers within the territory of the People's Republic of China (hereinafter referred to as the "Territory") shall be subject to the measures for VAT refund (exemption).

Article 2 For the purpose of the Administrative Measures for Applying the Tax Refund (Exemption) to Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated Value-added Tax (hereinafter referred to as the "Measures"), the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT, identified as the general VAT taxpayers refers to the entities and individuals within the Territory who provide taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT and adopt the general methods for tax assessment. Where VAT is paid on a consolidated basis, the payer is the head office approved by the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation for consolidated payment of VAT.

Article 3 The taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall be subject to the applicable scope provided by the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation.

The transport services for the legs or road segments corresponding to the waybill, bill of lading or passenger ticket with outbound starting point or terminal point are deemed as international transport services.

The transport services for the legs or road segments corresponding to the waybill, bill of lading or passenger ticket with starting point or terminal point in Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan are deemed as Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan transport services.

The carriage of passengers or goods from China to the special supervision areas and sites of the Customs of China, the carriage of passengers or goods from the special supervision areas and sites of the Customs of China to other areas within China or special supervision areas and sites of the Customs of China, and the research and development services and design services provided to entities located in the special supervision areas and sites of the Customs of China, shall be excluded from the applicable scope of the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT.

Article 4 The measures for applying the tax refund (exemption) to taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT include the measures for tax exemption, offset and refund and the measures for tax exemption and refund, and specific measures and computational formulas shall be subject to the provisions in respect of the tax refund (exemption) for exported goods and services specified in Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Value-added Tax and Consumption Tax Policies on Exported Goods and Services (Cai Shui [2012] No.39).

Where the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT subject to the measures for tax exemption, offset and refund concurrently exports goods and services and does not make separate accounting for them, the tax exemption, offset and refund shall be calculated together. In that case, the tax authority shall determine the amount of tax refund, exemption and offset based on the proportions of tax exemption, offset and refund for taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT and exported goods and services in the examination and approval

Article 5 The tax refund rate of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall be the VAT rate applicable to the corresponding services provided to entities within the Territory.

Article 6 The taxation bases for tax refund (exemption) in respect of the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall be determined on the basis of the following provisions:

  1. Taxation bases for tax refund (exemption) subject to the measures for tax exemption, offset and refund

(1) For the carriage of passengers by railway transportation, the taxation base shall be the actual transportation revenue after the liquidation conducted pursuant to the liquidation rules of Organization for Cooperation of Railways;

(2) For the carriage of goods by railway transportation, the taxation base shall be the actual transportation revenue after the liquidation conducted pursuant to the liquidation measures for railway transportation income for the transportation expenses as indicated on the freight bill with "Jing" (border) included in the name of "station of departure" or "destination (bureau)" and the directly relevant incidental expenses for international combined transport;

(3) For the carriage of goods or passengers by air transportation, the taxation base shall be the actual revenue after the liquidation by Accounting Centre of China Aviation if the carriages for the legs in the international transportation or the Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan transportation are accepted by more than one carrier; it shall be the revenue from rendering of the air transportation service if the carriages for the legs in the international transportation or the Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan transportation are accepted by one carrier;

(4) For other taxable services subject to the measures for tax exemption, offset and refund, the taxation base shall be the revenue from the rendering of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT.

  1. The taxation bases for tax refund (exemption) subject to the measures for tax exemption and refund shall be the amount as indicated in the VAT special invoice for purchasing the taxable services or the tax payment receipt of the People's Republic of China for delivery of tax payment.

Article 7 No VAT special invoice shall be issued for the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT subject to the measures for VAT refund (exemption).

Article 8 The provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall not declare the tax refund (exemption) for taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT until its qualification for export tax refund (exemption) is identified. Where the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT are provided before the identification of the qualification for export tax refund (exemption), tax refund (exemption) may be declared in accordance with provisions after the identification of the qualification for export tax refund (exemption).

Article 9 The provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall apply to the competent tax authority for identifying the qualification for export tax refund (exemption) in accordance with the following requirements:

  1. filling in and submitting the Application Form for the Identification of the Qualification for Export Refund (Exemption) and electronic data;

One of the bank accounts that are reported to the competent tax authority for recording when the tax registration is handled shall be filled in the "bank account for tax refund" of the Application Form for the Identification of the Qualification for Export Refund (Exemption).

  1. providing the originals and copies of the following materials according to the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT provided:

(1) Rendering of international transportation services. For the waterway transportation, the International Shipping Transportation License shall be provided; for the air transportation, Public Aviation Transportation Enterprise Operation License with the business scope thereof covering "international aviation transportation of passengers, goods and mails" or General Business License for Aviation Operation with the business scope thereof covering "official flight" shall be provided; for the highway transportation, Road Transportation Operation License and International Automobile Transportation Driving License with the business scope thereof covering "international transportation" shall be provided; for the railway transportation, the Business License for Enterprise as a Legal Person with the business scope thereof covering "licensed business item: railway transportation of passengers and goods" or other materials proving the qualification of rendering the railway transportation of passengers and goods shall be provided; for rendering air transportation service, the Business License for Enterprise as a Legal Person with the business scope thereof covering "commercial satellite launching services" or other materials proving the qualification of rendering the commercial satellite launching services.

(2) Rendering of Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation services. For the round-trip highway transportation service between Mainland and Hong Kong or Macau, the Road Transportation Operation License and the property proof of the transportation vehicles going directly to Hong Kong and Macau and carrying the Road Transportation License shall be provided; for the round-trip waterway transportation between Mainland and Hong Kong or Macau, the property proof of the vessels with the operating license for Hong Kong and Macau segment shall be provided; for the round-trip waterway transportation service between Mainland and Taiwan, the License for Water Transportation across Taiwan Strait and the property proof of the vessels carrying the Operation Certificate for Vessels Going across Taiwan Strait shall be provided; for the Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan air transportation services, the Public Air Transport Enterprise Operating License with the business scope thereof covering "international and domestic (including Hong Kong and Macau) transportation of passengers, goods and mails" or the General Business License for Aviation Operation with the business scope thereof covering "official flight" shall be provided; for the round-trip railway transportation between Mainland and Hong Kong, the Business License for Enterprise as a Legal Person with the business scope thereof covering "licensed business item: railway transportation of passengers and goods" or other materials proving the qualification of rendering the railway transportation of passengers and goods shall be provided.

(3) For the provision of international transport services and Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transport services using the transportation tools leased by voyage charter, time charter or wet lease, the voyage charter, time charter and wet-lease contract or agreement shall be provided.

(4) For the research and development services as well as design services provided to other countries, the Registration Certificate of the Technology Export Contract shall be provided.

  1. in addition to the above materials, for the goods or services exported by provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT without the identification of the qualification for export tax refund (exemption), the Filing Registration Form of Foreign Trade Operator stamped with the special seal for filing and registration and the Consignee and Consigner Declaration and Registration Certificate of the Customs of the People's Republic of China for Imported and Exported Goods along with a copy thereof shall be provided as well.

Article 10 Where an export enterprise providing taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT has identified qualification for export tax refund (exemption), the Application Form for Altering the Identification of the Qualification for Export Tax Refund (Exemption) and electronic data shall be filled in and submitted, and the materials corresponding to the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT set out in Article 9 shall be provided to apply to the competent tax authority for altering the identification of the qualification for export tax refund (exemption).

Article 11 Where the provider of the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT is required to alter the measures for VAT refund (exemption) in accordance with provisions, the competent tax authority shall conduct the liquidation for tax refund (exemption) in accordance with the current provisions and shall handle the alternation upon the tax settlement.

Article 12 For the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT provided by the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT, the declaration on the VAT payment and refund (exemption) shall be made with the competent tax authority within the tax declaration period of VAT payment in the following month after the sales income is provided by the accountant (or in the first month in the following quarter if VAT payment declaration is made on a quarterly basis, hereinafter the same).

After the collection of all relevant vouchers, the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall declare the tax refund (exemption) with the competent tax authority within the declaration period of various VAT payments from the following month after the sales income is provided by the accountant to April 30 of the next year. In case of any overdue declaration of tax refund (exemption), the competent tax authority shall refuse to accept the declaration. In the event of failure in declaring of tax refund (exemption) for the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT within the given period, the provider of taxable service eligible for zero-rated VAT shall pay the VAT in accordance with provisions.

Article 13 Provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT subject to the measures for tax exemption, offset and refund shall declare to the competent tax authority for VAT exemption, offset and refund in accordance with the following requirements:

  1. the Summary of Declaration for Tax Exemption, Offset and Refund and the exhibits thereof shall be filled and reported;

  2. the Value-Added Tax Payment Declaration Form for the current period shall be provided;

  3. the electronic data for official declaration of tax exemption, offset and refund shall be provided;

  4. invoices (approved by the competent tax authority, may only provide digital data, with the original vouchers kept for inspection) issued for providing taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall be provided;

  5. the following corresponding documents shall be provided according to the provided taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT:

(1) for those who provide international transportation services or Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation services, the Declaration Schedules of Tax Exemption, Offset and Refund for Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated VAT (International Transportation/Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan Transportation) (Appendix I) shall be filled in and submitted, and the following original vouchers and copies thereof shall be provided:

A. For transportation by water, air or highway, the manifestation of the carriage of goods or passengers in taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT, or other lists or vouchers which are capable of reflecting the original composition of the income shall be provided. Where transportation is provided by air, and the legs of international and Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation are responsible by different carriers, the Declaration Schedules of Liquidation Bills for International Air Transportation Income (Appendix II) shall also be provided.

B. For transportation by railway, in case of carriage of passengers, the international passenger combined transportation bills, the liquidation letter of Organization for Cooperation of Railways and the Declaration Schedules of Liquidation Letter for Income from International Railway Passenger Transportation (Appendix III) for taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall be provided; in case of carriage of goods, the International Railway Goods Transportation Income Liquidation Notice produced by railway income capital liquidation institution shall be provided, and the railway transportation enterprises in the departure region shall also provide the international railway combined transportation bills and the goods bills with "border" included in the name of "station of departure" or "destination (bureau)".

C. For the provision of international transportation services or Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation services using transportation tools leased by voyage charter, time charter or wet lease, the copy of voyage charter, time charter or wet-lease contracts or agreements shall be provided. Where time charter or wet-lease services are provided to foreign entities, and tax refund (exemption) shall be declared by the lessor in accordance with the provisions, the original voucher as described in Item A may be not necessary.

For the above original vouchers in Item A and Item B (excluding the Declaration Schedules of Liquidation Bills for International Air Transportation Income and the Declaration Schedules of Liquidation Letter for Income from International Railway Passenger Transportation), upon approval by the competent tax authority, the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT may provide electronic data only, with the original vouchers kept for inspection.

(2) for those who provide air transportation services, the Declaration Schedules of Tax Exemption, Offset, Refund for Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated VAT (Air Transportation) (Appendix IV) shall be filled in and submitted, and the following documents and original vouchers and copies thereof shall be provided:

A. contract concluded and entered into for providing air transportation services;

B. receipt vouchers for income received from the entities with whom the air transportation service contract has been signed and entered into; and

C. the Break-down List of Receipt of Business Amounts in Full for Air Transportation Services provided (Appendix V).

(3) for those who provide research and development and design services, the Declaration Schedules of Tax Exemption, Offset, Refund for Taxable Services Eligible for Zero-rated VAT (Research and Development Service/Design Service) (Appendix VI) shall be filled in and submitted, and the following documents and original vouchers and copies there of shall be provided:

A. copy of the Technology Export Contract Registration Certificate corresponding to the income from taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT;

B. the contract of research and development and design services signed and entered into with overseas entities;

C. receipt vouchers for income received from the overseas entities with whom the contract of research and development and design services has been signed and entered into; and

D. the Break-down List of Receipt of Business Amounts in Full for Research and Development Service/Design Service Provided to Overseas Entities (Appendix VII).

  1. other documents and vouchers required by the competent tax authority.

Article 14 Provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT subject to the measures for tax exemption and refund shall declare to the competent tax authority for tax exemption and refund in accordance with the following requirements:

  1. the Summary Declaration Form for the Export Tax Refund of Foreign Trade Enterprises shall be filled in and submitted;

  2. the Export Detailed Declaration Form of Taxable Services (research and development service/design service) Out-purchased by the Foreign Trade Enterprises (Appendix VIII) shall be filled in and submitted;

  3. the Schedules of Declaration Form of Goods Purchase for the Export Refund of Foreign Trade Enterprises that shall be filled in with the information about the special VAT invoice received for the research and development or design services corresponding to the foreign purchase; and

  4. the following original vouchers shall be provided:

(1) invoices for providing taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT shall be provided;

(2) for the purchase of research and development or design services from domestic entities or individuals for export, the special VAT invoices issued by the provider of taxable services shall be provided;

(3) for the purchase of research and development or design services from overseas entities or individuals for export, the tax payment receipt of the People's Republic of China for delivery of tax payment shall be provided; and

(4) the information and original vouchers listed in Sub-item 3, Item 5 of Article 13 shall be provided.

Article 15 Upon accepting the tax refund (exemption) declaration for taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT, the competent tax authority shall apply the export tax refund examination system for examination after verifying the accuracy of the following information manually. For the input of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT subject to the measures for tax exemption and refund, the export tax refund shall be examined by cross-auditing and assisted investigation and information examination all alike. If any question arises in the process of examination, the competent tax authority may make correspondent investigation or verification for input VAT special invoices of enterprises.

  1. For those who provide international transportation services, the competent authority may select several declaration records from the declaration made by providers of taxable service eligible for zero-rated VAT to examine the following contents:

(1) whether the international transportation service or Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation service declared is in compliance with the provisions on the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT;

(2) whether the income from the taxable service declared in the declaration records is less than or equal to the corresponding income from the international transportation service or Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation service recorded on the manifestation of carriage of goods or passengers in the declaration records.

(3) for the provision of international transportation services and Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation services using transportation tools leased by time charter, voyage charter or wet lease, the review shall focus on the contracts or agreements for the time charter, voyage charter or wet lease and whether the enterprises applying for tax refund (exemption) are subject to the provisions on taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT; and

(4) for the provision of international transportation services or Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan transportation services by railway transportation, the review shall focus on whether the names of "station of departure" or "destination (bureau)" on the goods bill provided include "border", and whether they correspond with the provided international railway combined transportation bills.

  1. For those who provide the research and development or design service, the following contents shall be examined:

(1) whether the research and development or design services declared by the enterprise are in compliance with the provisions on taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT;

(2) whether the counterparty executing the research and development and design contract is an overseas entity;

(3) whether the party paying the taxable service income is the overseas entity that has signed the research and development and design contract with the enterprise;

(4) whether the declared taxable service income is less than or equal to the amount received and obtained from the overseas entity that has signed the research and development and design contract with the enterprise; and

(5) where a foreign trade enterprise purchases research and development or design services from overseas for export, in addition to the contents on review mentioned above, the competent tax authorities shall also review whether the input tax amount for tax refund declaration corresponds with the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT.

Article 16 For a foreign trade enterprise, where the measures for tax refund (exemption) are changed from the measures for tax exemption and refund to the measures for tax exemption, offset and refund due to the exports of self-developed research and development services or design services, if the declared tax refund (exemption) records abnormal growth, and there occurs abnormal circumstance during the exports of goods and services, the competent tax authority may require the foreign trade enterprise to declare and submit the corresponding input vouchers of exported goods and services, and review the said vouchers in accordance with the provisions. If the competent tax authority finds out forged or untrue information in the input vouchers provided by the foreign trade enterprise, it will deal with it in accordance with relevant provisions of the Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Value-added Tax and Consumption Tax Policies on Exported Goods and Services (Cai Shui [2012] No.39).

Article 17 When the competent tax authority considers the export price of research and development or design services supplied by the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT is higher than normal, it shall be handled in accordance with Article 5 of the Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Certain Value-added Tax Policies for the Prevention of Tax Risks (Cai Shui [2013] No.112).

Article 18 Upon the review of the competent tax authority, if the tax refund (exemption) declared by the providers of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT has complete vouchers and satisfies the provisions on tax refund (exemption), the competent tax authority shall timely carry out the review and grant the approval, and handle the tax refund and offset transferring, with the tax refund amounts being paid from the central treasury on a unified basis.

Article 19 Where the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT is fraudulent in obtaining the state export tax refund, the tax authority shall deal with it in accordance with the provisions of the Circular of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues Concerning the Suspension of the Processing of Export Tax Refunds for Enterprises Which Commit Export Tax Refund Fraud (Guo Shui Fa [2008] No.32) and the Circular of the Ministry of Finance and the State Administration of Taxation on Certain Value-added Tax Policies for the Prevention of Tax Risks (Cai Shui [2013] No.112). The taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT rendered by the provider of the said taxable services during the period when the refund is suspended, the tax refund (exemption) shall not be declared and the VAT shall be paid according to relevant provisions.

Article 20 Were the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT provides taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT, if the application of zero VAT rate is waived, and tax exemption or paying VAT in accordance with relevant provisions are chosen, the provider shall declare and submit the Statement for the Waiver of Application of Zero Value-added Tax Rate (Appendix IX) to the competent tax authority and go through the filing procedures. Within 36 months since the first day of the next month after filing, the taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT provided by the enterprise shall not be declared VAT refund (exemption).

Article 21 The competent tax authorities shall strengthen the analysis and monitoring over the tax refund (exemption) applicable to zero VAT rate of the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT.

Article 22 The Measures require that the electronic data in the declaration form declared by the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT to the competent tax authority shall be generated and declared through the export tax refund (exemption) declaration system. Before the generation of the information and the upgrading of the declaration function of the export tax refund (exemption) declaration system, the electronic data needing declaration may be substituted by written materials temporarily.

The export tax refund (exemption) declaration system may be downloaded for free from the website of the State Administration of Taxation, or is available from the competent tax authority for free.

Article 23 The Measures require the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT to provide the competent tax authority with original documents and copies thereof, and the copies provided by the said provider shall be noted with "in line with the original" and with seals of the enterprise. The competent tax authority shall return the originals back and keep the copies upon examining the correspondence of them.

Article 24 The Measures shall come into force as of January 1, 2014, subject to the date when the provider of taxable services eligible for zero-rated VAT provides the said services and the sales income is recorded by the accountant.
