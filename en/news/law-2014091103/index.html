Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning Simplifying and Consolidating the Value-added Tax Levy Rates

Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2014] No.36

June 27, 2014

According to the decision of the State Council on simplifying and consolidating the value-added tax levy rates, the relevant issues are hereby announced as follows:

Article 1 "The tax authority of the place where the business is conducted shall levy the tax at the rate of 6%" set forth in the Circular of the State Administration of Taxation on the Administration on the Special VAT Invoice of Businesses with a Fixed Establishment that Temporarily Conducts Outward Business (Guo Shui Fa [1995] No.87) shall be revised as "the tax authority of the place where the business is conducted shall levy the tax at the rate of 3%".

Article 2 "Value-added tax shall be levied at the rate of 4%" set forth in Article 1 of the Circular of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning the Value-added Tax and Business Tax Levied on Auction Proceeds Obtained by Auction Companies (Guo Shui Fa [1999] No.40) shall be revised as "value-added tax shall be levied at the rate of 3%".

Article 3 "A reduced levy rate of 2% from the original 4% under the simple value-added tax collection policy" set forth in Item 1 of Article 1 of the Circular of the State Administration of Taxation on Administrative Issues concerning the Simple Value-added Tax Collection Policy (Guo Shui Han [2009] No.90) shall be revised as "a reduced levy rate of 2% from the original 3% under the simple value-added tax collection policy".

Article 4 "The taxable amount shall be calculated at a levy rate of 6% under the simple value-added tax collection policy" set forth in Article 2 of the Official Reply of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning the Value-added Tax Policies on Supplying Non-clinical Blood (Guo Shui Han [2009] No.456) shall be revised as "the taxable amount shall be calculated at a levy rate of 3% under the simple value-added collection policy".

Article 5 "A reduced levy rate of 2% from the original 4% under the simple value-added tax collection policy may be applied" set forth in the Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning Value-added Tax on General Taxpayers' Sale of Their Used Fixed Assets (Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation [2012] No.1) shall be revised as "A reduced levy rate of 2% from the original 3% under the simple value-added tax collection policy may be applied".

Article 6 For taxpayers who apply a reduced levy rate of 2% from the original 3% under the simple value-added tax collection policy, the sales and taxable amount thereof shall be determined according to the following formulae:

Sales = Tax-inclusive sales / (1 + 3%)

Taxable amount = Sales × 2%

Item 1 of Article 4 of the Circular of the State Administration of Taxation on Administrative Issues concerning the Simple Value-added Tax Collection Policy (Guo Shui Han [2009] No.90) shall be repealed.

Article 7 The Announcement of the State Administration of Taxation on Issues concerning Simplifying and Consolidating the Value-added Tax Levy Rates shall come into force as of July 1, 2014.

It is hereby announced.

国家税务总局

2014年6月27日
