Circular of the State Administration of Taxation on Printing and Issuing the Annual Report on the Affiliated Transactions of Enterprises of the People's Republic of China

Guo Shui Fa [2008] No. 114

December 5, 2008

The Offices of the State Administration of Taxation and local taxation bureaus at all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government and cities specifically designated in the state plan,

With a view to implementing the Law of the People's Republic of China on Enterprise Income Tax and its Regulations for the Implementation, the State Administration of Taxation has formulated the Annual Report on the Affiliated Transactions of Enterprises. When an enterprise submits its annual enterprise income tax returns to the competent tax authorities, the Annual Report on the Affiliated Transactions of Enterprise shall be attached in accordance with the provisions of Item 1 of

Article 43 of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Enterprise Income Tax.

The Annual Report on the Affiliated Transactions of Enterprises of the People's Republic of China (see Appendix) is hereby printed and issued to you, and the tax authorities at all places shall timely perform the printing, issuing, learning, training and software modification and other work involving the Report.

Appendix: Annual Report on the Affiliated Transactions of Enterprises of the People's Republic of China
