Guiding Opinions of the General Office of the Ministry of Commerce on Doing a Good Job in the Dissolution and Liquidation of Foreign Investment Enterprises

2008/05/05

Guiding Opinions of the General Office of the Ministry of Commerce on Doing a Good Job in the Dissolution and Liquidation of Foreign Investment Enterprises

Shang Fa Zi [2008] No. 31

May 5, 2008

The competent commerce authorities in all provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities directly under the Central Government, cities specifically designated in the state plan and the Xinjiang Production and Construction Corps.,

The Measures for Liquidation of Foreign Investment Enterprises (hereinafter referred to as the Measures), since its implementation in 1996, has played an active role in guaranteeing the smoothness of liquidation of foreign investment enterprises. Along with gradual improvement of the legal system in China and the uniform management over the domestically-invested enterprises and foreign-invested enterprises, the State Council abolished the Measures on January 15, 2008. The opinions on the dissolution and liquidation of foreign investment enterprises subsequent to the abolishment of the Measures are hereby notified as follows:

Article 1 The future dissolution and liquidation of foreign investment enterprises shall be implemented in accordance with the Company Law and the laws and administrative regulations on foreign investment. Where there are special provisions in the laws and administrative regulations on foreign investment but there are no relevant detailed provisions in the Company Law, the special provisions shall prevail.

Article 2 Where a foreign investment enterprise is terminated in accordance with the Items (2), (4), (5) and (6) of Paragraph 1 of Article 90 of the Regulation on Implementation of the Law on Sino-foreign Equity Joint Ventures or Items (2), (4) and (5) of Paragraph 1 of Article 48 of the Implementing Rules of the Law of People's Republic of China on Sino-Foreign Co-operative Joint Ventures or Items (2), (3) and (6) of Paragraph 1 of Article 72 of the Implementing Rules of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Foreign-invested Enterprises, it shall submit the examination and approval authority with an application letter for advance dissolution, the resolution on advance dissolution of the enterprise as made by the power body of the enterprise (Board of Directors, Board of Shareholders or Shareholders' General Meeting, hereinafter the same), the approval certificate and the business license of the enterprise.

Where the investor of a Sino-foreign equity joint venture or a Sino-foreign contractual joint venture unilaterally files a dissolution application in accordance with Item (3) of Paragraph 1 of Article 90 of the Regulation on Implementation of the Law on Sino-foreign Equity Joint Ventures or Item (3) of Paragraph 1 of Article 48 of the Implementing Rules of the Law of People's Republic of China on Sino-Foreign Co-operative Joint Ventures, the investor shall submit the examination and approval authority with an application for advance dissolution and the effective judgment or verdict made by the jurisdictional people's court or arbitration institution, in which the circumstance provided for by any of the aforesaid two Items shall be included.

The examination and approval authority shall, after receiving the application for dissolution and other relevant materials, grant the document on approval of enterprise dissolution within ten working days, and add the information concerning the approval of enterprise dissolution in the national foreign investment enterprise examination and approval management system. The enterprise shall, within 15 days since the date on which the dissolution is approved, establish a liquidation group and start the liquidation by law.

Article 3 Where some of the shareholders of a foreign investment enterprise require dissolving the enterprise in accordance with Article 183 of the Company Law of the People's Republic of China, they shall file an application directly to the people's court with jurisdiction.

Article 4 The liquidation group shall, within the period of liquidation, pay all the taxes on the enterprise. Subsequent to the end of the liquidation, the liquidation group shall prepare a liquidation report, and send the report to the examination and approval authority upon confirmation of the power body of the enterprise, and simultaneously turn in the approval certificate to the examination and approval authority for write-off. Subsequent to receipt of the liquidation report and the approval certificate, the examination and approval authority shall complete entry of related information about enterprise termination in the national foreign investment enterprise examination and approval management system. The system will automatically generate an acknowledgement of receipt, and the enterprise shall conduct the write-off procedures at the departments of taxation, customs and foreign exchange, and apply for deregistration at the company registration authority.

Article 5 Where the special liquidation has already started prior to the abolishment of the Measures, the special liquidation procedures can be continuously conducted, and the liquidation report prepared by the liquidation committee by law shall have the legal effect.
