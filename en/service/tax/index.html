Tax and Business Advisory Services

Tax and Business Advisory Service

Denchi Advisory Co., Ltd. (Denchi) can provide your company with comprehensive tax and business advisory services, including, without limitation to;

Enterprise Investment, Tax, Customs, Foreign Exchange Advisory Services

As an enterprise, it is a huge challenge as well as a great opportunity to figure out an efficient model, which enables the corporation to meet the corporate objectives and business operational needs and manage the investment, tax, customs and foreign exchange businesses. Denchi provides enterprises with thorough advisory services, including replying to general questions, analyzing particular situation and/or updating the latest regulations and training. As a result, the enterprises can concentrate more on their developments on the major businesses.

Corporate Tax Incentives Advisory Services

We focus on helping corporations from various industries to identify the applicable tax incentives and gain cash benefits; eventually lower the corporations’ actual tax rates and increase profits. Our services include relative analysis regarding to tax incentives application, documents preparation and applying and negotiating on their behalf with government /authorities.

Corporation Tax Compliance Advisory Services

As corporations continually expand their markets, the requirements of tax preparation and compliance are getting more and more complex. Our professional experts can assist your company to accomplish the managerial target at minimum cost.

Corporate Tax Health Check and Due Diligent Advisory Services

Doing corporation tax health check or due diligence is like a thorough body examination to the corporation. During the examination or investigation, managements or counterparties are aware of their tax compliance situation. In the light of the situation that Chinese Tax Agencies gradually strengthens the tax collection and administration, it is more and more valuable and essential to the management to conduct effective risk control, tax health check and due diligence.

Corporation Internal Tax Control Advisory Services

China has its unique economic environmental and developing characteristics. For the corporate managements who operating businesses in China, tax risk-control is one of the most essential problems. Our professionals are experienced, dedicated and sincere to cooperate with your company’s IT, Human Resource and Financial Accounting departments. We focus on helping your company to improve the tax risk-control system by thorough evaluation and effective design.

Employee Dispatching and Tax Services

Denchi employees dispatching and tax service provides thorough services regarding to the enterprises dispatching their employees overseas, including compliance services of applying work permit and residence visa, planning and applying personal income tax in China on their behalf.

Tax Treaties Negotiation

Members of Denchi and CTAC include people working on tax treaties negotiation services professionally; they can assist your company from all aspects in the tax treaties negotiation business.

Proxy Service

It will be a hard time for the enterprises to deal with the verification and modification procedures required by different levels of government authorities. We try our best to provide your company with proxy services in terms of tax, customs, foreign exchange, foreign companies’ investment setup and modification and so on at a reasonable low cost.
